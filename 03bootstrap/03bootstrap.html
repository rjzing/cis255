Zingsting's Computers

Here at Zingsting's Computers, we are dedicated to our customers. We will never tell you what you want to hear, but what you need to hear. Our employees are among the most experienced people when it comes to anything technology. We sell components, custom built computers, and used computers, and with our price match guarantee you never have to wonder if that's the price out there.

Components

We only sell components that we use in our own builds, making sure we have the best knowledge out there. So you never have to guess how good the product is when purchasing it.

We also have demo computers around the store; however, we cannot have every possible build variation out there but we can get you the closest thing to what your possible new computer will be. We also have a youtube channel where we test different builds. Since we are sponsored by the best known companies, we can take requests of what you'd like to see next on youtube.

Custom Built Computers

If you do not feel comfortable building your own computer, we can direct you to the best possible computer that fits your needs and build it.

We gather the parts directly from the manufacturer, so you never have to worry about spending too much money on the components, keeping the overall build price as cheap as possible.

Used Computers

Do not think badly of our used computers, they may be used, but we always test everything that ever comes in, also, most of the used computers we sell are either our personal old builds or builds we have done with previous customers.

If we sell you a used computer, we give it our limited two year warranty. If any component goes bad in the first two years of owning it, we replace it with a brand new one.
